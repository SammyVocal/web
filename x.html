⚡ Sameer Kumar Singh

Sammy Verified

Sameer Kumar Singh, known as Sammy, is an independent Indian singer, composer, and live performer from Ara, Bihar. With a soulful voice blending classical, ghazal, sufi, and modern romantic styles, Sammy has captivated over 400K listeners. His viral covers, like Paro and Aadat, have amassed 100M+ views, earning him 370K+ Instagram followers and a thriving YouTube community.

Musical Journey

Sammy’s passion for music sparked in 3rd grade when he performed a patriotic song at school on August 15th. Gifted a ₹5,500 harmonium by his father, he found his calling. This moment marked the start of a journey fueled by dedication and self-belief.

From humble beginnings, Sammy built a professional home studio with two harmoniums, two guitars, and a piano, funded by his own earnings. This creative hub powers his music, transforming a single harmonium into a legacy of soulful melodies.

Achievements

Sammy’s covers, including Mujhe Peene Do and Tu Hai Ke Nahi, have gone viral, collectively surpassing 100 million views. His versatile style—spanning classical to trending songs—resonates deeply, building a digital following of over 370K on Instagram and a growing YouTube audience.

Vision

Hailing from Ara, where Bhojpuri reigns, Sammy carved a niche in Hindi music, winning hearts nationwide. His journey from a small town to recognition by over 4 lakh listeners embodies the power of passion. Sammy aims to inspire as an independent artist who breaks barriers through music.
